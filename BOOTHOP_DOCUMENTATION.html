1 — Platform Overview
BootHop runs two independent products from a single Next.js codebase. Each has its own authentication, database tables, email flows, and admin interfaces. They share infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel, Stripe, SendGrid).

P2P  Marketplace — Hoopers & Booters

  • Hoopers (senders) post delivery requests
  • Booters (travellers) carry goods along their route
  • Custom JWT auth — email OTP, cookie: boothop_session
  • 9-stage match state machine + 3 side states
  • Stripe Identity KYC (no Stripe payments — manual admin)
  • 21 transactional emails across full lifecycle
  • 3 core cron jobs (auto-match, reminders, expiry)

B2B  Business Platform — Logistics

  • Three service tiers: Express Booking, Carrier Network, Priority Partner
  • Separate OTP auth — cookie: boothop_biz_session
  • Stripe checkout for all B2B payments
  • Automated job-engine with carrier matching & escalation
  • 34 scheduled cron jobs across the full platform
  • Dedicated admin dashboards per tier

3 — Business Platform (B2B) Product 2
Auth: separate OTP flow, cookie boothop_biz_session. OTP format: 4 digits + 1 letter = 5 characters (e.g. 4827A). Remember-me cookie boothop_biz_remember (30 days) skips OTP on trusted devices. Three independent service tiers share the same auth API, each has its own branded sign-in page.
