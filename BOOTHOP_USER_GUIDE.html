What is BootHop?

BootHop is a platform that connects people who need to send parcels with people who are already travelling the same route. The Traveller earns money from spare luggage space. The Sender pays far less than a traditional courier. BootHop holds the money safely in the middle and only releases it once both sides confirm the delivery is done.
📦

The Sender

Also called: Hooper

Needs a parcel delivered. Posts a request and pays when matched with a Traveller.

✈️

Already making a journey. Carries a parcel for earnings — no extra trip needed.

The Sender Journey — Step by Step

"I need to get something delivered"

1
Create a Free Account

Sign up at boothop.com with your email address. Takes less than 2 minutes. You receive a £20 welcome credit automatically applied to your first booking.

2
Post Your Delivery Request

Fill in a simple form: where the parcel is going, when it needs to arrive, what's inside, how much it's worth, and what you're willing to pay. BootHop automatically checks whether your item can be sent on that route.

⚠️ Be honest about what's inside. BootHop uses AI to screen items. Prohibited goods will be blocked automatically.
3
Get Matched with a Traveller

BootHop searches for Travellers heading the same way around the same time. You'll receive an email when a match is found. You can also browse Travellers manually and express interest.

4
Agree on Price

Review the Traveller's route, date, and star rating. You can negotiate the price by sending a counter-offer. Once both sides agree, the match moves forward.

⚠️ Africa routes (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, etc.): A BootHop team member reviews the match before you're asked to pay. This takes a few hours during business hours.
5
Pay Securely — Money is Held, Not Released Yet

You pay via a secure card page. Your money goes into a holding account (escrow) — it is NOT given to the Traveller yet. Your card is authorised but only charged when delivery is confirmed.

✅ Your £20 welcome credit is deducted automatically here.
6
Receive Contact Details & Arrange Handoff

Once payment is in place, you receive the Traveller's phone number and email. Arrange a convenient time and place to hand over the parcel before their journey.

✅ Both of you also receive a unique QR barcode by email — your tracking tool throughout the delivery.
7
Track Your Delivery Live

Visit the tracking link sent by email or scan your QR barcode. You can see exactly where your parcel is. You can also "ping" the Traveller to request a location update.

8
Confirm Receipt

When your parcel arrives, click "Confirm Delivery" in your BootHop dashboard. One tap is all it takes.

9
Done — Leave a Rating

Once both you and the Traveller have confirmed, the Traveller is paid. You're invited to leave a star rating and comment. Your feedback helps future Senders choose reliable Travellers.

The Traveller Journey — Step by Step

"I'm travelling and I want to earn money from my spare luggage space"

1
Create a Free Account

Sign up at boothop.com with your email address.

2
Verify Your Identity (Required Before Earning)

Before you can carry anything, you must complete identity verification: upload a government-issued ID and add your bank account details. This is handled securely through Stripe — the same technology used by Amazon and Deliveroo.

⚠️ Verification typically takes 24–48 hours. You cannot accept matches until this is complete. This protects both you and the Sender.
3
Post Your Travel Route

Fill in a simple form: where you're travelling from and to, when you're departing, how much spare space you have, and how much you want to earn.

4
Get Matched & Accept

BootHop finds Senders who need something delivered on your route. You receive an email with the details — parcel type, weight, price offered. You can accept, decline, or counter-offer.

✅ You only carry what you're comfortable with. You are never forced to accept a match.
5
Collect the Parcel

Once the Sender has paid, you both receive each other's contact details and your QR barcodes. Arrange collection of the parcel before your journey.

6
Carry & Log Updates

As you travel, use your barcode to log checkpoints in the BootHop app: parcel collected → in transit → arrived at destination → delivered. The Sender can see these updates live.

⚠️ If you go 48 hours without logging any update, BootHop will contact you and the Sender. Always keep your status current.
7
Deliver & Confirm

Hand the parcel to the recipient and confirm delivery in your BootHop dashboard.

8
Get Paid

Once both you and the Sender confirm delivery, payment is transferred to your bank account — typically within 1–2 business days.

💰 Typical earnings: £20–£50 (domestic) · £50–£120 (UK–Europe) · £85–£320 (UK–Africa)

What Can (and Cannot) Be Sent

BootHop uses intelligent screening to check all items automatically when a delivery request is posted. Here is a plain-English guide to what is and isn't permitted.

✅ Generally Permitted
  • 👕 Clothing and personal items
  • 💻 Electronics (phones, tablets, laptops)
  • 📄 Documents and paperwork
  • 🎁 Gifts and household goods
  • 🍱 Non-perishable food & groceries
  • 💍 Jewellery (subject to value limits)
  • 👟 Shoes and accessories
  • 🧴 Personal care products
  • 📚 Books and educational materials
  • 🧸 Toys and children's items
❌ Never Permitted
  • 🔫 Weapons or ammunition of any kind
  • 💊 Illegal drugs or controlled substances
  • 💣 Explosives or flammable materials
  • 🐾 Live animals
  • 🎭 Counterfeit or stolen goods
  • 💸 Large cash amounts (varies by route)
  • ☢️ Radioactive or hazardous materials
  • 🚫 Child exploitation material
  • 💀 Human remains
  • 🎰 Gambling devices (certain routes)

Safety & Trust — 8 Layers of Protection

Every single delivery on BootHop is protected by eight automatic safety layers. You don't need to do anything — these all run in the background.

🔍
1 Identity Verification Every Traveller must verify their government ID before they can carry anything. Powered by Stripe Identity — the same system banks use.
🖥️
2 Device Fingerprinting BootHop silently identifies the device being used. Known fraud devices are blocked before any harm is done.
🚨
3 Account & IP Ban Checks Any account or internet address previously flagged for fraud is automatically blocked from accepting new matches.
🧠
4 Fraud Risk Scoring Every match is scored across five factors — identity, past behaviour, transaction value, route risk, and device signals. High-risk matches are blocked automatically.
📦
5 Item Screening Every item is checked against a prohibited goods list and route-specific rules. Banned or restricted items are caught before a match is confirmed.
🤖
6 AI Customs Check Artificial intelligence classifies each item and checks it against international customs rules for that specific country pair.
🔒
7 Stripe Secure Payment Payments are processed by Stripe with bank-level 3D Secure authentication. Money is held in escrow — not released until delivery is confirmed.
✅
8 Dual Delivery Confirmation Both the Sender AND the Traveller must confirm delivery before any money moves. Neither party can release funds alone.
